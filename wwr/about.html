Our mission is to provide the most thrilling and safe rafting experiences. We believe in creating memories that last a lifetime, surrounded by the beauty of nature. Join us to discover your own high ground.

History

Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate river guides, High Ground Odyssey started as a small operation with a big dream: to share the unparalleled joy of whitewater rafting. Over the years, we've grown into a premier adventure company, but our core values of safety, fun, and respect for the environment have remained unchanged. We've guided thousands of guests down breathtaking rivers and continue to be a leader in the outdoor adventure industry.
